Normal Distribution
Parameters of the Normal Distribution

Topic of the module

Understand the effect of changing the "location parameter" \(\mu\) and the "scale parameter" \(\sigma\) on the shape of the probability density function (pdf) and the cumulative probability density function (cdf) of the normal distribution, i.e., for \(Y\) with, $$ \begin{align} Y &\sim N\left(\mu, \sigma\right), \end{align} $$ where \(\mu\) is the expected value, \(\text{E}\left(Y\right)\), or, the "location parameter", and \(\sigma\) is the standard deviation \(\sqrt{\text{Var}\left(Y\right)}\), or, the "scale parameter", of the normal distribution.

Illustration

Change the parameters and see the effect on the shape of the pdf and cdf of the normal distribution.
